## 2) The Problem

**Key Points**

**Speaker Notes**

**Market Pain Points**

- 40% of creative revisions due to unclear decision logic

- Freelance/boutique studios locked out of enterprise deals due to accountability gaps

**One‑liner**: The industry makes high‑stakes creative calls without decision systems.

## 3) The Breakthrough

**Key Points**

- Deterministic decision engine (not probabilistic)
- Not an analysis tool
- Outputs anchored in evidence

**Speaker Notes**
Our engine converts visual signals into structured creative decisions using rule-based inference, not black-box ML. Every decision includes: (1) the signals that triggered it, (2) the logic applied, (3) confidence level, (4) risk trade-offs, and (5) recommended action. Identical inputs always produce identical outputs—accountability by design.

**Technical Differentiation**

- Deterministic vs. stochastic (no guessing, no re-runs)
- Governance-first architecture (traceability built in, not added)
- Versioned outputs (can audit any past decision instantly)

**One‑liner**: We deliver decisions, not just data.

## 4) How It Works

**Pipeline**

1. **Input**: Visual media (photos, footage, design comps)
2. **Signal Extraction**: Cinematic patterns (composition, color psychology, motion grammar)
3. **Deterministic Inference**: Rule engine applies creative logic
4. **Decision Output**: Structured recommendation with reasoning
5. **Governance Layer**: Versioning, traceability, audit trail
6. **Delivery**: Exportable decision deck with embedded rationale

**Speaker Notes**
The pipeline is intentionally calm—no flashing dashboards or overhyped visualizations. A creative director gets a single, decisive recommendation with the evidence behind it. The tool gets out of the way. Studio can adopt it in 2 days; enterprise rollout in 2 weeks.

**Time to Decision**: 4–6 minutes per asset (vs. 2–3 days manual)

**One‑liner**: A clear path from signal to creative direction.

## Innovation Uniqueness

### What Makes It Different

- **Deterministic** (not probabilistic): Identical inputs always produce identical outputs
- **Governance-first**: Traceability and versioning built into the core, not bolted on
- **Decision-focused** (not analysis): Outputs are actionable directives, not recommendations
- **Traceable by design**: Every decision linked to signal provenance without exposing raw logs

### Key Differentiators

1. **No black-box AI**: Rule-based inference engine is explainable and auditable
2. **Versioned outputs**: Can instantly retrieve and audit any past decision
3. **Enterprise-grade**: Meets compliance requirements (GDPR, audit trails, data residency)
4. **Studio-friendly**: Minimal UI; gets out of the way of creative directors
